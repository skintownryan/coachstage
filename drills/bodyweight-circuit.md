title: Bodyweight Strength Circuit
stage: "2"
type: physical
time: 25 min
players: Any
equipment: None
summary: A structured circuit of bodyweight exercises developing whole-body strength and movement quality. Appropriate for this age as strength gains are neural — no muscle bulk, just better movement.
objective: Develop whole-body movement quality and neural strength through fundamental bodyweight patterns.
setup: Mark 5 stations with cones. Athletes rotate on a signal.
instructions:
  - Station 1 — press-ups, 30 seconds. Focus on straight body position.
  - Station 2 — squat jumps, 30 seconds. Land quietly and controlled.
  - Station 3 — plank hold, 30 seconds. Squeeze everything tight.
  - Station 4 — reverse lunges, 30 seconds alternating legs.
  - Station 5 — broad jumps, 30 seconds. Maximum distance each jump.
  - Rest 90 seconds between full circuits. Complete 2–3 circuits.
coaching: Quality over quantity at every station. Stop and reset technique rather than allowing poor reps. Praise effort and form not number of reps completed.
variations: Add a medicine ball. Increase to 45 second stations. Partner mirror drills at each station.
