title: Animal Movement Circuit
stage: "1"
type: physical
time: 15 min
players: 4–20
equipment: Cones to mark stations
summary: Athletes move between stations copying different animal movements — bear crawl, crab walk, frog jump, bunny hop. Develops strength, coordination, and body awareness through play.
objective: Build fundamental movement competency and body awareness in a playful imaginative format.
setup: Mark 5–6 stations around the space. Each station has an animal movement assigned to it.
instructions:
  - Bear crawl — on all fours, opposite arm and leg move together.
  - Crab walk — facing upward on hands and feet, walk sideways.
  - Frog jumps — squat low, jump forward landing in squat.
  - Bunny hops — two feet together, hop continuously.
  - Inchworm — hands walk out to plank, walk feet to hands, repeat.
  - Spend 30 seconds at each station then rotate.
coaching: Encourage imagination — make the animal sounds, get into character. This is play, not exercise. Correct form gently and briefly.
variations: Add a penguin waddle or flamingo balance. Race between stations. Let athletes invent new animals.
