title: Four Goal Chaos
stage: "2"
type: game
time: 25 min
players: 8–16
equipment: 4 sets of small goals or cone pairs, bibs
summary: Two teams can score in any of four goals scattered around the pitch. Develops vision, scanning, communication, and constant real-time decision-making.
objective: Develop scanning ability, communication, and rapid decision-making in an unpredictable environment.
setup: Place 4 small goals randomly across a large area. Split into 2 equal teams with bibs.
instructions:
  - Both teams can attack and defend any of the four goals.
  - Normal rules apply — no goalkeeper required.
  - Coach can change goal positions between rounds.
  - Play 5 minute rounds with a short rest and question period between.
coaching: Resist the urge to organise them. The chaos is the point — let them problem-solve. Use rest periods to ask questions not give answers.
variations: Three goals per team. Limit touches in attacking zones. Count goals scored from distance as double.
