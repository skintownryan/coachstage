title: Traffic Lights
stage: "1"
type: game
time: 10 min
players: 6–20
equipment: None
summary: A classic movement game where the coach calls colours to control speed. Develops listening, reaction time, and basic locomotion in a fun pressure-free format.
objective: Develop listening skills, reaction time, and basic locomotion patterns through play.
setup: Mark out a large playing area. Athletes spread out and move freely in any direction.
instructions:
  - Coach calls "Red" — everyone stops immediately.
  - Coach calls "Amber" — everyone walks.
  - Coach calls "Green" — everyone runs freely.
  - Add variations — "Blue" means hop on one leg, "Purple" means skip.
  - Last one to respond correctly sits out for 10 seconds then rejoins.
coaching: Keep the pace unpredictable and fun. Praise quick reactions with enthusiasm. Make errors feel completely safe — laugh about them together.
variations: Add directional calls. Use cones as colour zones. Let an athlete be the caller.
