time: 20 min
players: Any
equipment: Agility ladder, cones
summary: A progression of agility ladder footwork patterns developing coordination, foot speed, and movement efficiency. This age group is at peak capacity for acquiring complex movement patterns.
objective: Develop advanced footwork patterns, foot speed, and coordination during the peak motor learning window.
setup: Lay out 2 agility ladders side by side. Athletes queue at one end with adequate space between reps.
instructions:
  - Pattern 1 — two feet in each rung moving forward.
  - Pattern 2 — one foot in each rung, high knees.
  - Pattern 3 — lateral shuffle, two feet in each rung moving sideways.
  - Pattern 4 — in-in-out-out pattern moving forward.
  - Pattern 5 — icky shuffle — 1 in 2 in 1 out pattern laterally.
  - Walk back recovery between each run.
coaching: Rhythm and accuracy matter more than speed at first. Speed comes naturally with repetition. Correct footwork errors immediately but briefly — demonstrate rather than just explain.
variations: Add a sprint at the end of the ladder. Vary the exit direction — athlete must react to a coach signal. Combine two patterns in sequence.
