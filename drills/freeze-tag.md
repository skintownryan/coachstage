title: Freeze Tag
stage: "1"
type: game
time: 10 min
players: 8–25
equipment: Bibs
summary: Classic tag game where frozen players can be freed by teammates. Builds running, dodging, spatial awareness, and introduces basic cooperation.
objective: Develop evasion skills, spatial awareness, and a simple team concept through joyful play.
setup: Mark a large area with cones. Assign 2–3 taggers who wear bibs.
instructions:
  - Taggers try to freeze players by touching them.
  - Frozen players stand still with arms outstretched.
  - Free players can unfreeze frozen ones by high-fiving them.
  - Rotate taggers every 2–3 minutes.
  - Celebrate unfreezing moments loudly.
coaching: Keep it joyful. Step in as a tagger yourself occasionally to raise excitement. Celebrate the unfreezer as a hero every time.
variations: Two-touch freeze. Safe zones. Add a countdown before frozen players sit down.
