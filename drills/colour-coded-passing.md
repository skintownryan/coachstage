title: Colour Coded Passing Game
stage: "2"
type: game
time: 20 min
players: 9–18
equipment: Bibs in 3 colours, balls
summary: Three teams in the same space each maintaining their own possession. Athletes must find their own colour teammate while avoiding the other two teams. Develops vision, scanning, and passing under pressure.
objective: Develop scanning, quick decision-making, and passing accuracy in a congested environment.
setup: Three equal teams each wearing different coloured bibs. All three teams share the same space simultaneously.
instructions:
  - Each team maintains their own possession independently.
  - Pass only to your own colour.
  - All three games happen simultaneously in the same space.
  - Count consecutive passes — first team to 10 wins the round.
  - Losing team does a short fun forfeit.
coaching: The congestion is the challenge — do not separate the teams. Encourage athletes to move to create space and call for the ball. Scan constantly.
variations: Limit to two touches. Add a fourth neutral team. Allow stealing from other colour teams.
