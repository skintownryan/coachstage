title: Position Specific Decision Rondo
stage: "3"
type: game
time: 25 min
players: 8–14
equipment: Balls, cones
summary: A possession rondo where each player is assigned a positional role and must make decisions consistent with that role. Develops positional intelligence and understanding of team game models.
objective: Develop position-specific decision-making within a shared team game model and language.
setup: Standard rondo square. Each player assigned a positional identity — striker, midfielder, defender, goalkeeper.
instructions:
  - Play standard possession rondo rules.
  - Each player must make decisions consistent with their assigned role.
  - Defenders win the ball and recycle it safely. Strikers take risks and play forward.
  - Coach provides one individual cue per player per round only.
  - Rotate positions every 10 minutes.
coaching: Focus the debrief on whether decisions matched the positional role. Build a shared vocabulary — as a striker in this situation what did you want to do and why?
variations: Overload specific positions. Add a game model rule such as always attempt to play forward first. Link to recent match video footage in the debrief.
