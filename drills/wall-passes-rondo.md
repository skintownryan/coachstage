title: Wall Passes Rondo
stage: "2"
type: skill
time: 20 min
players: 8–16
equipment: Balls, cones
summary: A small-sided possession game where outer players maintain possession against pressure from players in the middle. Develops passing accuracy, movement off the ball, and decision-making.
objective: Develop quick passing, support play, and decision-making under pressure.
setup: Mark a 10m x 10m square with cones. Start with 6 outer players and 2 in the middle.
instructions:
  - Outer players maintain possession.
  - Two middle players press to win the ball.
  - If the middle wins possession, the player who lost it swaps in.
  - Progress to 5v2 then 4v2 as skill level improves.
  - Count consecutive passes as a team goal.
coaching: Ask why questions after each sequence — why did you pass there, who was free? Keep interventions short. Let the game do the teaching.
variations: Limit to two touches. Play one-touch only. Add a neutral player always on the side of the team in possession.
