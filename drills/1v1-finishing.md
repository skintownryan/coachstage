title: 1v1 Finishing Under Pressure
stage: "3"
type: skill
time: 25 min
players: 8–16
equipment: Goals, balls
summary: Attackers face defenders in realistic 1v1 situations finishing at goal. Develops attacking decision-making, finishing technique, and the ability to beat a defender in a high-pressure environment.
objective: Develop attacking decision-making, finishing technique, and confidence in 1v1 situations against live opposition.
setup: Mark a channel 15m wide and 20m long with a goal at one end. Attackers queue at the far end, defenders start at the goal.
instructions:
  - Attacker receives a pass and attacks the defender.
  - Defender tries to delay and prevent a shot.
  - If attacker scores or defender wins possession the round ends.
  - Rotate attacker and defender roles after each rep.
  - Coach feeds the ball to start each rep.
coaching: Encourage attackers to be decisive — commit to a direction and go. Reward bravery over caution. Discuss which attacking moves worked and why.
variations: Add a second defender. Give attacker 4 seconds only. Play to a small target goal to emphasise accuracy.
