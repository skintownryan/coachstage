title: Sharks and Minnows
stage: "1"
type: game
time: 15 min
players: 8–25
equipment: Bibs, cones
summary: One or two sharks try to tag minnows crossing from one side to the other. Tagged minnows become sharks. Develops evasion, decision-making under pressure, and explosive acceleration.
objective: Develop explosive acceleration, evasion, and basic decision-making in a high-energy game.
setup: Mark two safe zones at opposite ends of the space. One or two athletes are sharks wearing bibs.
instructions:
  - Minnows start in one safe zone.
  - On the signal all minnows try to cross to the other safe zone.
  - Sharks try to tag minnows in the open space.
  - Tagged minnows become sharks.
  - Last minnow standing wins and becomes the first shark next round.
coaching: Encourage minnows to use fakes and changes of direction. Sharks should communicate with each other. Praise clever movement not just speed.
variations: Sharks can only hop. Minnows carry a ball. Add a middle safe island that fits only 3 minnows at a time.
