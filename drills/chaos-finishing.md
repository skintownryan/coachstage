title: Chaos Finishing Circuit
stage: "4"
type: skill
time: 25 min
players: 8–16
equipment: Multiple balls, goals, cones
summary: A high-tempo finishing circuit where athletes must score immediately after completing a physical challenge. Develops finishing composure under fatigue and simulates the real demands of scoring late in a match.
objective: Develop finishing composure, technical execution, and confidence when physically fatigued.
setup: Place a goal with goalkeeper. Set up 4 cones at different angles and distances. Coach has supply of balls.
instructions:
  - Athlete completes a physical challenge — 5 press-ups, sprint around a cone, or 3 headers.
  - Immediately on completion coach serves a ball for the athlete to finish.
  - Athlete gets one touch to control and one touch to shoot.
  - Rotate through all four positions at different angles and distances.
  - Score one point per goal, compete against previous personal best.
coaching: Simulate match intensity — urgency, pressure, encouragement from teammates. Celebrate composure under fatigue as much as the goal itself. Analyse misses constructively and briefly.
variations: Add a time limit from completion to shot. Include a defender who arrives 2 seconds after the ball. Use different ball deliveries — bouncing, aerial, driven.
