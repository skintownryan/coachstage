title: Pressure Skill Circuit
stage: "3"
type: skill
time: 25 min
players: Any
equipment: Sport-specific equipment, cones, stopwatch
summary: Athletes perform their key technical skill under progressively increasing time pressure and physical fatigue. Develops the critical ability to execute technique when tired and under stress.
objective: Execute sport-specific technical skills under physical and time pressure to simulate real competition demands.
setup: Set up 4 stations. 45 seconds work, 15 seconds transition between stations.
instructions:
  - Station 1 — technical skill at own pace as a warm-up baseline.
  - Station 2 — same skill with a specific time target such as 10 repetitions in 30 seconds.
  - Station 3 — same skill immediately after 6 shuttle runs to induce fatigue.
  - Station 4 — same skill under direct pressure from a partner defending.
coaching: Make the technical breakdown under fatigue explicit and educational. Awareness of how pressure affects their performance is itself a valuable skill. Discuss it openly.
variations: Increase the fatigue stimulus. Add a cognitive task such as simple mental maths between reps. Introduce crowd noise or distractions to increase pressure.
