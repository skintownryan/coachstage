title: Advanced Decision Rondo
stage: "4"
type: skill
time: 25 min
players: 8–14
equipment: Balls, cones
summary: A high-intensity possession rondo with complex constraints that demand rapid decision-making, technical execution under pressure, and positional awareness at near-match intensity.
objective: Develop rapid decision-making and technical execution at high intensity in a realistic possession context.
setup: Standard rondo square 12m x 12m. 5v2 or 6v2 depending on numbers. All players at near-match intensity.
instructions:
  - Outer players maintain possession against two pressing players.
  - Each outer player has a maximum of two touches — one touch encouraged.
  - Ball out of the square means the last two to touch swap with the pressers.
  - Count maximum consecutive passes as a group target.
  - Progress the constraint — add a third presser to increase difficulty.
coaching: Intervene minimally. Let the game problem-solve for you. Use short pauses to ask tactical questions then restart quickly. Keep intensity high throughout.
variations: Add a condition that every fifth pass must be played through the middle. Play with one touch only in the final progression. Use a smaller square to increase pressure.
