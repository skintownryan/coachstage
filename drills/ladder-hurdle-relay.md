title: Ladder and Hurdle Relay
stage: "1"
type: skill
time: 20 min
players: 6–20
equipment: Agility ladder, low hurdles or cones
summary: Simple agility stations where athletes run through a ladder and over hurdles before tagging the next person. Develops footwork and basic coordination.
objective: Introduce agility ladder footwork and hurdle coordination through simple team relay.
setup: Set up a short agility ladder and 3–4 low hurdles or cones in a line. Split athletes into small teams.
instructions:
  - One athlete at a time runs through the ladder with one foot per space.
  - Jump over each hurdle in sequence.
  - Sprint back and tag the next teammate.
  - Rotate through several times.
  - Progress to two feet per space or lateral ladder patterns.
coaching: Focus on fun and rhythm not perfect footwork. Celebrate completion not speed. Keep teams small so athletes get many turns quickly.
variations: Different ladder patterns. Vary hurdle heights. Add a ball carry or dribble through the course.
