title: Periodised Conditioning Block
stage: "4"
type: physical
time: 35 min
players: Any
equipment: Cones, stopwatch, bibs
summary: A structured high-intensity interval conditioning session designed around the specific physical demands of competition. Develops sport-specific aerobic and anaerobic capacity at appropriate volumes for this age group.
objective: Develop sport-specific aerobic and anaerobic conditioning at volumes appropriate for late adolescent athletes.
setup: Mark a rectangular grid 30m x 20m. Athletes work in pairs to monitor each other.
instructions:
  - Block 1 — aerobic base. 4 minutes continuous work at 70 percent effort. 2 minute rest.
  - Block 2 — threshold work. 3 x 2 minutes at 85 percent effort. 90 second rest between reps.
  - Block 3 — anaerobic capacity. 8 x 30 seconds maximum effort. 90 second rest between reps.
  - Complete total blocks matching the session goal for that training week.
  - Athletes track their own effort levels using perceived exertion 1–10.
coaching: Teach athletes to self-regulate intensity honestly. Discuss perceived exertion openly — learning to read your own body is a lifelong athletic skill. Adjust volumes based on recent competition load.
variations: Use sport-specific movement patterns rather than just running. Adjust work-to-rest ratios based on the sport's physical demands. Add decision-making tasks during conditioning blocks.
