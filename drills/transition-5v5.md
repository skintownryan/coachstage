title: Transition 5v5
stage: "3"
type: game
time: 30 min
players: 10+
equipment: Goals, bibs
summary: A small-sided game with a built-in transition rule — all players must cross the halfway line before their team can score. Develops pressing, defensive organisation, and attacking transition speed.
objective: Develop defensive shape, pressing triggers, and attacking transition decision-making.
setup: Standard small-sided area. Mark a halfway line clearly. Two equal teams.
instructions:
  - When a team wins possession all players must get over the halfway line before they can score.
  - The defending team presses immediately to prevent the transition.
  - Play 5 minute rounds with a tactical discussion between each round.
  - Losing team completes a fun physical challenge — not punitive.
coaching: Pause play to highlight defensive shape. Ask attackers what options they had. Ask defenders what shape they were trying to hold. Questions not answers.
variations: Change the recovery line distance. Add a third team waiting to play the winner. Count transition goals scored within 5 seconds as double.
