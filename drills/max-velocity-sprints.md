title: Maximum Velocity Sprint Development
stage: "4"
type: physical
time: 30 min
players: Any
equipment: Cones, timing gates if available
summary: A structured session developing top-end sprint speed through flying sprints and acceleration work. Athletes at this stage are physically ready to push true maximum velocity training safely.
objective: Develop maximum sprint velocity using appropriate recovery protocols to ensure quality of every effort.
setup: Mark distances at 10m, 20m, and 40m on a flat surface. Full recovery between every effort is non-negotiable.
instructions:
  - Warm-up with progressive runs at 60 percent, 75 percent, and 90 percent effort over 40m.
  - Flying 30s — take a 20m build-up run then sprint at absolute maximum for 30m.
  - Complete 3 sets with full 3 minute recovery between each.
  - Cooldown with easy jogging and thorough stretching.
  - Track times if gates are available and chart progress over weeks.
coaching: Full recovery is completely non-negotiable — quality over quantity every time. Cue relaxed fast movement — jaw loose, hands relaxed, shoulders down. Tense sprinting is slower sprinting.
variations: Add resisted sprints with a sled or band on some reps for contrast training effect. Use timing gates to give athletes objective performance data to track.
