title: Rapid Wall Repetition
stage: "2"
type: skill
time: 20 min
players: Any
equipment: Ball, wall or rebounder
summary: Athletes perform rapid continuous repetitions against a wall developing touch, reaction time, and technical consistency under fatigue conditions.
objective: Build technical consistency and quick reactions through high-volume deliberate repetition.
setup: Athletes stand 3–5 metres from a solid wall with a ball each.
instructions:
  - Strike or throw the ball against the wall continuously.
  - Goal is to maintain technique as speed gradually increases.
  - Work for 30 seconds then rest for 15 seconds.
  - Complete 5–8 rounds per session.
  - Give one technical focus point per round only.
coaching: Watch for the moment fatigue causes technique to break down — that is the key learning moment. Name it and discuss it. One cue per round maximum.
variations: Vary the distance from the wall. Alternate feet or hands each rep. Add targets marked on the wall to aim for.
