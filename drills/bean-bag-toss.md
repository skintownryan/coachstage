title: Bean Bag Toss and Chase
stage: "1"
type: skill
time: 15 min
players: 4–16
equipment: Bean bags or balls
summary: Athletes throw a bean bag, chase it, pick it up and repeat. Develops throwing mechanics and basic hand-eye coordination through self-paced exploration.
objective: Develop basic throwing patterns and hand-eye coordination in a pressure-free environment.
setup: Athletes stand behind a line with a bean bag each. Large open space required.
instructions:
  - Athlete throws the bean bag as far as they can.
  - They sprint and collect it.
  - Return to the line and repeat.
  - Progress to underarm, overarm, then with the non-dominant hand.
  - Add targets on the ground to aim for.
coaching: Focus on movement quality not distance. Praise effort generously. Avoid over-correcting — let them discover through repetition.
variations: Partner throws. Target practice with hoops. Throw and touch the floor before collecting.
