title: Blob Tag
stage: "1"
type: game
time: 12 min
players: 10–30
equipment: None
summary: Two athletes link hands forming a blob and work together to tag others. Tagged athletes join the blob. Develops cooperation, spatial awareness, communication, and joyful chaos.
objective: Develop basic cooperation, communication, and spatial awareness through a high-energy team game.
setup: Large open space clearly marked. Two athletes start as the blob holding hands.
instructions:
  - The blob moves together holding hands and tries to tag free athletes.
  - Tagged athletes join the blob by taking a hand at either end.
  - The blob can only tag with their free outside hands.
  - When the blob reaches 4 athletes it splits into two blobs of 2.
  - Continue until all athletes have been caught.
coaching: Encourage the blob to communicate and strategise — corner athletes, split to cover space. Celebrate clever blob teamwork as much as individual evasion.
variations: Multiple starting blobs. Blob must stay in a specific zone. Free athletes can free others by crawling through the blob's legs.
